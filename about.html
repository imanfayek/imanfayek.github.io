Hello!

I'm Iman. I love making things and experimenting with various mediums of doing so.

I've been in the games industry since 2016 and have worked as a software engineer, artist, designer, and project manager.

In 2022, I founded a non-profit organization called Sunbloom Media Arts Center. Our goal is to grow the games industry into a safer and more equitable space by fostering community and providing opportunities for people of color.

I'm excited to be a part of the IGDA Foundation's 2024 GDC Next Gen Leaders program!
