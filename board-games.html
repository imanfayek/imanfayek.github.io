Supernova Paradox

COPYRIGHT © 2017 Iman Fayek

This game is about a young scientist & an old drifter racing against each other to save the world!The goal is to collect the correct ingredients to power a massive machine. There are six possible endings to the game, depending on the ingredients chosen. Initial concept & story by me, as well as game tiles & art.

 

Created in three weeks with Rogelio Espinoza & Frank Young.
