Supernova Paradox

This game is about a young scientist & an old drifter racing against each other to save the world! Building the gameboard as you go, you must collect the various ingredients to power a massive machine. I was responsible for the general structure and mechanics of the game, as well as the narrative design, creating multiple story endings. I also created the game tiles with the help of a die cutter.
