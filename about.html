Hello!

I'm Iman. I love making things and experimenting with various mediums of doing so.

I've been working in the games industry since 2017 and, as a second-gen immigrant and a brown woman, I have always felt a bit out of place, both in appearance and in the stories and ideas I wish to share. I significantly value people over product and I'm committed to making the industry a better place for marginalized devs.

I'm currently building a non-profit organization that boosts racially marginalized devs, but I'm also open to creative opportunities, particularly in product, game, and UX design.

You can reach me here: hello@imanfayek.com
