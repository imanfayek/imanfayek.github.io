UNTITLED DOG GAME

The game's initial playable character is a little chihuahua, on a mission to retrieve a stolen item from the villainous King Cat. As the game progresses, characters will be unlocked (primarily other dog breeds). The player's abilities will be dependent on the character chosen for gameplay.

While I am new to modeling game assets, I have been solid modeling for engineering design for a couple of years. Here's a look at some current and past projects!
