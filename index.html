Hi, I'm Iman!

I love creating interactive, expressive experiences and experimenting with various mediums of doing so.

I just started a non-profit organization that boosts racially marginalized game devs and would love to talk to anyone interested interested in getting involved.

I'm also open to creative opportunities, particularly in product, game, and UX design.
