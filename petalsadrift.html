Petals Adrift

Other Devs: Zejun Yuan

Artists: Lydia Valdez, Janice Lee, Julie Huang, Karolyn Moses

Unity3D

Petals Adrift is a fast-paced 2D beat-matching puzzle game! Play as a butterfly peacefully collecting flower petals in the forest. Avoid sinister obstacles (watch out for those evil, evil frogs) and solve puzzles to impress the forest inhabitants. Make music with mushrooms!

Check us out in this Intel article!

I served as Creative Director, Gameplay Programmer, Producer, and Musician.

As Creative Director, I pitched the initial game concept to a panel of industry judges and kept the game's creative vision intact through production. I designed all systems in the game, as well as all levels, providing detailed design documents to the team during development.

As Gameplay Programmer, I developed the code structure and pair-programmed with Jun to implement player movement and puzzle logic. I ensured all animations and movement occurred on beat, without the use of a plugin.

As Producer, I ensured that the game stayed within a reasonable scope and that production maintained a steady pace, without causing the team to crunch. I maintained a dev blog on tumblr, showing insight into the design and development of the game.

And as the game's Musician, I crafted the game's soundtrack using GarageBand, ensuring that each track had a strong beat for the player to beat-match.
