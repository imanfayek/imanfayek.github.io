Train Jam 201920

Unity3D

A short little game showing the small, menial moments on a train. Train Jam is a game jam that takes place on a train ride from Chicago to the Bay Area on the days preceding GDC (Game Developer's Conference). In 2019, the train was delayed ~20 hours and we all lose our minds a little :) At one point, the staff referred to us as Train Jam 2020, hence the title.
