Pebble Lake VR

Virtual Reality

HTC Vive

Unity3D

Pebble Lake is a VR rock-skipping simulator made for the 2017 Global Game Jam. When faced with the theme of "waves," we decided to build something that we all experienced growing up - making waves on a lake with a handful of pebbles! Game design was an overall collaborative effort, with each of us contributing additional pieces. I helped with audio design and finding the 3D models, as well as building the terrain in Unity.
