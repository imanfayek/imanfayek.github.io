Brosaurus Wrecks!

Other Devs: Rogelio Espinoza, Zheng Guo, Kireeti Vetagiri, Roy Huang

Artists: Elizabeth Chee, Amanda Yau, Jonathan Cheung, Sam Glover, Janice Lee, Lydia Valdez

VO: Sam Reha

Virtual Reality

HTC Vive

Unreal Engine 4

Brosaurus Wrecks! is a game about embracing your inner dino and getting swole. In the game, you take on the body of Brosaurus, a frat bro turned giant monster, who must maintain their physique by destroying surrounding cities. The game is played in room-scale VR, allowing a more immersive experience.

What'd I do?

- I employed the scrum methodology to assign weekly tasks, analyzing and taking into consideration prior progress to determine future courses of action. I also ensured all critical tasks were completed on time and that everyone received any necessary help.

- I ensured that the game stayed within a reasonable scope and that production maintained a steady pace. In addition, I conducted and observed all playtesting sessions, designed/coded/produced all copy for the game's website, put together promotional materials, and made the game's trailer.

- I created the game's energetic soundtrack using GarageBand.
