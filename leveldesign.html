Original Maps

This is a level designed to suit the default mechanics of the GameMaker demo. While the purple background is from the original demo, I supplied the pixel art. The main character is my character and the dog bones only make sense if you know that I have a dog. His name is Roscoe.

A map created for the 2D platformer that Petals Adrift evolved from. In this game, you played as a singing flower on the hunt for its missing petals. At point 3 on the map, you jumped on the back of a butterfly and guided it's flight with your song. At 4, you sang a special note towards the ground, causing a platform to pop up.
