Brosaurus Wrecks!

Virtual Reality

HTC Vive

Unreal Engine 4

Brosaurus Wrecks! is a game about embracing your inner dino and getting swole. In the game, you take on the body of Brosaurus, a frat bro turned giant monster, who must maintain their physique by destroying surrounding cities. The game is played in room-scale VR, allowing a more immersive experience.

I joined Team Brosaurus just as they were starting the production phase of the game, filling the roles of Producer and Development Director. As the former, I kept the game moving forward, ran and analyzed playtesting sessions, and crafted our online presence. As the latter, I served as the game's task master, managing five developers and six artists, ensuring that all critical tasks were completed, and analyzing previous progress to determine future courses of action. Towards the middle of production, we found out that our sound designer would no longer be able to contribute to the project, so I stepped in with a borrowed Macbook (PC4lyfe) and created the game's soundtrack. This was my first foray into music and I am super proud of what I accomplished!

As Producer:

  • Ensured that the game stayed within a reasonable scope and that production maintained a steady pace.
  • Observed all playtesting sessions, recording notes and prioritized areas of improvement.
  • Designed, coded, and produced all copy for the game's website, set up social media accounts, put together promotional materials, and made the game's trailer.

As Development Director:

  • Created and assigned tasks weekly, analyzing and taking into consideration progress made in previous weeks.
  • Employed the scrum methodology during production, using Jira and Trello as tools.
  • Ensured that all critical tasks were completed on time and that everyone received any necessary help.

As Sound Designer / Musician:

  • Crafted the game's soundtrack, using GarageBand to create workout inspired songs for each level and all menu screens.
  • Compiled all SFX to be used in game.
