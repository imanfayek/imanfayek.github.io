Hate

Other Devs: Zejun Yuan

This is an abstract expression of the word hate. Play as the star of your own universe, destroying anything different in your path with the help of your crew. Working on a team of two, I was responsible for bringing the Star & the Star Minions to life, as well as implementing the color lerping used in the Star and Circles. I also did the sound design for this game and it still makes me uncomfortable.
