UNTITLED DOG GAME

Some of my favorite games growing up were 2D platformers, such as Super Mario Brothers, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, and Lion King. Of course, I decided to create my own game based on this style. This game will be built using C# and Unity!

The game's initial playable character is a little chihuahua, on a mission to overthrow the villainous King Cat. As the game progresses, characters will be unlocked (primarily other dog breeds). The player's abilities will be dependent on the character chosen for gameplay.

There will be items that can be collected, such as a bone (think Mario coins) and an apple (possible projectile!).

Villains will be comprised of two categories: bosses and minions. For human characters, the sprite will be chosen at random, allowing for unique variations of the game.

Levels and worlds will be created with a mixture of tiles and free form pixel art.
