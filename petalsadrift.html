Petals Adrift

Unity3D

Petals Adrift is a fast-paced 2D beat-matching puzzle game! Play as a butterfly peacefully collecting flower petals in the forest. Avoid sinister obstacles (watch out for those evil, evil frogs) and solve puzzles to impress the forest inhabitants. Make music with mushrooms!

Check us out in this Intel article!

This game started as a platformer, evolved into a different platformer, and then found its final form. As a team of two developers (with four artists from San Jose State), we really nailed down rapid prototyping! However, once we brought our game to the vertical slice stage, we decided to halt production in order to pursue VR opportunities. Now, production has resumed and the first demo is set to go live this October.

As Creative Director:

  • Pitched the initial game concept to a panel of industry judges and was selected for production.
  • Designed all systems in the game, from game mechanics to puzzles.
  • Provided detailed design documents for the team during development.
  • Designed all levels and assembled them in the Unity engine.

As a Gameplay Programmer:

  • Developed the code structure and implemented player movement and puzzle logic.
  • Programmed all animations and movement to occur on beat, without the use of a plugin.

As Producer:

  • Ensured that the game stayed within a reasonable scope and that production maintained a steady pace.
  • Maintained a dev blog on tumblr, showing insight into the design and development of the game.

And as Musician:

  • Crafted the game's soundtrack, using GarageBand to create simple, hip-hop inspired songs, ensuring that each track had a strong beat for the player to beat-match.
